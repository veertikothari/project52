Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. A Chameleon's eyes can swivel 180 degrees
  2. Chameleons like warm, tropical environments like the rain forest
  3. Males usually have horns and spikes, where females don't

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons always lay at least 2 eggs with each one being a male and a female
  2. A full grown pigeon has 10,000 feathers
  3. Pigeons have been known to live over 30 years

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  3. Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

Interesting Facts about Bears

  1. The Koala is not a bear.
  2. Baloo, from The Jungle Book, is a sloth bear.
  3. Bears eat mostly meat and fish, but some bears also eat plants and insects.
